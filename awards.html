Awards & Recognition

To encourage active learning, knowledge sharing, and active participation throughout the five-day workshop, various awards and recognitions were presented during the valedictory ceremony. Participants were evaluated based on quiz performance, technical discussions, interaction during sessions, and overall engagement throughout QMI 2025.

🏆 QMI Young Scholar Award

Award for Outstanding UG & PG Students

Presented to undergraduate and postgraduate students who demonstrated exceptional enthusiasm, outstanding quiz performance, active participation during technical sessions, and excellent overall engagement throughout the workshop.

🏆 QMI Research Excellence Award

Award for Research Scholars

Presented to research scholars who exhibited excellence in research discussions, academic interactions, collaborative learning, and active involvement during all workshop activities.

🏆 QMI Distinguished Professional Award

Award for Faculty Members & Industry Professionals

Presented to faculty members and industry delegates for their active contribution, participation, knowledge sharing, and commitment towards advancing interdisciplinary research.

📜 Certificate Distribution

Participation Certificates

Certificates of Participation were distributed to all registered participants in recognition of their successful completion of the five-day workshop.

🎁 Memento Presentation

Honouring Invited Speakers

Invited speakers, keynote speakers, and distinguished guests were honoured with commemorative mementos in appreciation of their valuable contribution to QMI 2025.

📸 Award Winners

Group Photograph

Award winners gathered together with the organizing committee and invited dignitaries to commemorate the successful conclusion of the Awards and Recognition ceremony.
