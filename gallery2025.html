QMI 2025 Gallery

Day 1

Inaugural Ceremony, Registration, Welcome Address, and Technical Sessions.

The workshop commenced with registration and the inaugural ceremony. Distinguished guests, speakers, faculty members, and participants gathered to mark the beginning of QMI 2025. The day featured keynote lectures and technical sessions introducing the themes of quantum mathematics, imaging, and Industry 4.0 technologies.

Day 2

Expert Lectures and Interactive Technical Sessions.

Day 2 focused on advanced topics delivered by invited experts. Participants actively engaged in discussions and explored emerging research directions in quantum computing, mathematical modelling, and imaging technologies.

Day 3

Hands-on Activities, Technical Discussions, and Networking.

The third day encouraged collaborative learning through interactive activities, technical discussions, and networking opportunities among participants, researchers, and faculty members.

Day 4

Refreshments, Quiz Competition, and Participant Engagement.

Day 4 featured participant engagement activities including refreshments, networking interactions, and a quiz competition. These activities provided an opportunity for participants to reinforce their learning and collaborate with peers.

Day 5

Expert Sessions, Valedictory Function, and Prize Distribution.

The final day included expert lectures, technical presentations, prize distribution, certificate distribution, and the valedictory function. The workshop concluded with a vote of thanks and a group photograph commemorating the successful completion of QMI 2025.

Organizing Committee

The organizing committee, faculty members, research scholars, and volunteers of the Department of Mathematics, VIT Chennai, played a vital role in the successful conduct of QMI 2025.

Participants Group Photograph

Participants from various universities, research institutions, and industries joined together for a group photograph, celebrating the successful completion of the workshop.

Food & Refreshments

Refreshment Breaks and Networking Sessions

Throughout QMI 2025, participants were provided with refreshments and meals during scheduled breaks. These sessions created a relaxed environment for networking, academic discussions, and interactions among speakers, faculty members, research scholars, and students. The refreshment breaks served as valuable opportunities for collaboration and knowledge exchange beyond the formal technical sessions.
