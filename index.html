About SAS

The School of Advanced Sciences (SAS) at Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) Chennai, comprises Department of Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry. It offers the following programmes: Ph.D, M.Sc Data Science, M.Sc Physics, M.Sc Chemistry.
