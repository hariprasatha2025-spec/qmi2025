ABOUT QMI 2026

The 2nd National Workshop on “Quantum Mathematics and Biomedical Imaging for Intelligent Healthcare 5.0 (QMI 2026)” aims to bring together researchers, clinicians, industry experts, faculty members, and students to explore emerging advances in biomedical imaging, healthcare analytics, artificial intelligence, and quantum technologies. As healthcare systems increasingly rely on data-driven decision-making, there is a growing need for innovative mathematical frameworks and intelligent computational methods capable of addressing complex clinical challenges. Biomedical imaging modalities such as MRI, CT, PET, SPECT, ultrasound, and digital pathology generate vast amounts of heterogeneous data that require sophisticated analysis for accurate disease diagnosis and treatment planning. Recent developments in machine learning, deep learning, and quantum-inspired approaches have opened new possibilities for disease characterization, medical image interpretation, clinical decision support, and healthcare innovation. QMI 2026 is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of the entire healthcare imaging ecosystem from mathematical foundations and clinical interpretation to AI-driven analytics, quantum opportunities, ethical considerations, intellectual property, and translational healthcare technologies. The workshop will feature expert lectures, hands-on sessions, panel discussions, and interactive activities delivered by leading academicians, clinicians, industry professionals, and technology innovators. The workshop seeks to foster interdisciplinary collaboration and inspire future research at the intersection of mathematics, biomedical imaging, artificial intelligence, and intelligent healthcare systems, contributing to the vision of Healthcare 5.0 and the broader goals of technology-enabled healthcare transformation.

About VIT

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) was founded in 1984 as Vellore Engineering College, a self-financing inistitution dedicated to providing quality higher education. In 2001, the Union Ministry of Human Resource Development conferred university status on the institution under Section 3 of the University Grants Commission (UGC) Act, 1956. VIT is led by its visionary founder and Chancellor, Dr. G. Viswanathan, a former Parliamentarian and Minister in the Government of Tamil Nadu. In recognition of his exceptional contributions to education, Dr. G. Viswanathan was awarded an honorary doctorate by West Virginia University (USA) in 2009 and the State University of New York (USA) in 2024 With a commitment to delivering world-class education on par with international standards, VIT continually adopts innovative approaches to teaching and research. Many of our students, working in international organizations and universities, deliver high-quality solutions and conduct research that bring esteem to India and make us proud of our legacy. At VIT, the pursuit of excellence in education and research is complemented by a dynamic ecosystem of innovation, research and global engagement. As we advance steadily, we remain steadfast in our mission to mature leaders and innovators who can make a meaningful impact on the world.
